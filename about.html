About Our Chapter

The American Cancer Society at Innovation Academy is a student-led organization committed to raising awareness, supporting cancer research, and uniting our community in the fight against cancer.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — to eliminate cancer as a major health problem through education, community action, and fundraising. We strive to support patients, survivors, and families while promoting awareness of prevention and early detection.

Our Story

The ACS at IA chapter was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate students determined to bring awareness and compassion to the fight against cancer. Through volunteerism, education, and advocacy, we’ve built a network of hope that continues to grow each year.
